Office Work

Office work refers to the professional or clerical duties performed in an office environment, which can include tasks like data entry, communication via phone and email, creating documents and spreadsheets, and managing administrative processes. It is also used to describe the role or position itself, or a company's official presence in a location.

Labour Work

Labour work" refers to the human effort, both mental and physical, used to produce goods and services, often for pay. The term is most commonly used in the context of the labor market, which includes everyone who is employed, unemployed but seeking work, and the employers who hire them. The labor force includes those who are currently employed and those actively looking for a job, while those not seeking work (like some students or unpaid family workers) are typically excluded from official labor statistics.
